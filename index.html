GENEA Workshop 2021

Generation and Evaluation of Non-verbal Behaviour for Embodied Agents

The GENEA (Generation and Evaluation of Non-verbal Behaviour for Embodied Agents) Workshop 2021 aims at bringing together researchers that use different methods for non-verbal-behaviour generation and evaluation, regardless of application area, and hopes to stimulate the discussions on how to improve both the generation methods and the evaluation of the results. We invite all interested researchers to submit a paper related to their work in the area and to participate in the workshop.
